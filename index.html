How it works

No sign-up, no server, no tracking — everything runs in your browser.

01 — DATA

Reads local JSON

Projects, companies and materials load once from static JSON files and are cached by your browser for offline use.

02 — EXPLORE

Search & filter

Fast keyword search, category filters, and a natural-language bar like "completed bridges in Norway" — all client-side.

03 — SAVE

Favorites stay local

Star a location and it's remembered in your browser's storage. Nothing leaves your device.

04 — MANAGE

Edit in the dashboard

Add, edit, import or export entries as JSON — a drop-in replacement path for a future real API.

Part of the IndustrCons ecosystem

The map cross-links to the rest of the IndustrCons platform — costing, documentation and AI assistance.
